Our wide ranges of dental services include:

Dental Services

Dental Extraction

this procedure is on a tooth that can be seen in the mouth. For a simple extraction, the dentist loosens the tooth with an instrument called an elevator.

Dental Fillings

Fillings help restore teeth damaged by decay back to their normal function, and can prevent further decay.


Braces Install

If you have crooked teeth and/or a misaligned bite (an underbite or overbite), there are a variety of treatments that can help straighten teeth, including braces and retainers.

Children Clinic

Our office provides specialized dentistry for children and adolescents in a warm, caring and "child-friendly" environment.



Tooth Cleaning

Professional dental cleanings (dental prophylaxis) are usually performed by Registered Dental Hygienists.
